Pricing

Model A: Rental Price Range Prediction

Moderation

Model B: Listing Review And Lifecycle

Predicts whether a listing should be approved, reviewed, or rejected, then maps it to a lifecycle status.

Run the form to see moderation output, gender policy effects, and lifecycle status.
